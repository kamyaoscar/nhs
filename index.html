Welcome to Namungoona High School

Established in 1999, Namungoona High School has been a trusted leader in secondary education for over two decades. Located 5 kilometers along Hoima Road (100 meters off Nakibinge Stage) in Kampala, we operate under the visionary leadership of our Director, Hon. Ssemaganda Ronald.

Our primary mission is to impart affordable, qualitative, and skilled knowledge through reliable means, ensuring every student achieves their highest potential. We envision becoming a leading center of academic excellence in secondary education across the region.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of academic excellence in secondary education.

Our Mission

To impart affordable, qualitative, and skilled knowledge through viable and reliable means for sustainable learners.
